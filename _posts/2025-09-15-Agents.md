### NPCpy
**Code**: [https://github.com/NPC-Worldwide/npcpy](https://github.com/NPC-Worldwide/npcpy)<br>
```
from npcpy.npc_compiler import NPC
simon = NPC(
          name='Simon Bolivar',
          primary_directive='Liberate South America from the Spanish Royalists.',
          model='gemma3:4b',
          provider='ollama'
          )
response = simon.get_llm_response("What is the most important territory to retain in the Andes mountains?")
print(response['response'])
```

#### Post-Training Strategies
AgentGym-RL supports a suite of mainstream online RL algorithms: **PPO, GRPO, RLOO, REINFORCE++**.<br>
Beyond online RL, AgentGym-RL also supports a broad range of complementary training paradigms: **SFT, DPO, AgentEvol**.<br>
